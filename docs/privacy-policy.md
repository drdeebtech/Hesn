# Privacy Policy — حصن (Hesn)

_Last updated: 2026-06-01_

حصن (Hesn) is an azkar (أذكار) app for morning and evening remembrances. We
designed it to respect your privacy completely.

## نعربية (Arabic)

نستمع فقط لنعرف أنك أنهيت الذكر. لا نسجّل صوتك، ولا نحوّله إلى نص، ولا يخرج من
جهازك. التطبيق يعمل دون اتصال بالإنترنت، ولا يجمع أي بيانات شخصية، ولا يحتوي على
حسابات أو تتبّع. كل الإعدادات وحالة التقدّم تُحفظ محليًا على جهازك فقط.

## English

### What we collect

**Nothing.** Hesn does not collect, store off-device, or transmit any personal
data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

### Microphone

During an azkar session the app uses the microphone **only** to detect that you
have spoken and then paused (voice-activity detection by audio level). The app:

- **does not record** audio to any file;
- **does not convert** audio to text (no speech recognition);
- **does not send** any audio off your device.

The microphone is active **only** while a session is running and is released
immediately afterward.

### Data storage

Your settings (reminder times, voice-detection and hands-free preferences) and
your daily completion status are stored **locally on your device only**, using
the operating system's standard local storage. They never leave your device and
are removed if you uninstall the app.

### Network

The app makes **no network connections**. It works fully offline.

### Notifications

The app schedules local reminder notifications on your device at the times you
choose. These are generated on-device; no push servers or third parties are
involved.

### Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this page.
